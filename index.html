üìãClinical Treatment Protocols

Cardiac Protocols

Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS)
MD Approved
Indications: Cardiac arrest, ventricular fibrillation, pulseless ventricular tachycardia
Contraindications: Terminal illness with DNR orders
Medications:
  • Epinephrine 1mg IV/IO every 3-5 minutes
  • Amiodarone 300mg IV/IO for refractory VF/VT
  • Atropine 1mg IV/IO for bradycardia (if applicable)
Acute Myocardial Infarction
MD Approved
Indications: Chest pain with STEMI criteria, elevated troponin
Medications: Aspirin 324mg PO, Nitroglycerin 0.4mg SL, Morphine 2-4mg IV PRN

Endocrine Protocols

Hypoglycemia Management
MD Approved
Indications: Blood glucose <60 mg/dL with altered mental status
Standing Orders:
  • D50 25-50ml IV push (if IV access available)
  • Glucagon 1mg IM/IN (if no IV access)
  • Recheck BGL in 10-15 minutes

Pediatric Protocols

Pediatric Anaphylaxis
Specialist Required
Indications: Severe allergic reaction with respiratory distress or shock
Weight-Based Dosing:
  • Epinephrine: 0.01mg/kg IM (max 0.3mg) or EpiPen Jr. 0.15mg
  • Albuterol: 2.5mg nebulized
  • Diphenhydramine: 1mg/kg IV/IM (max 50mg)
